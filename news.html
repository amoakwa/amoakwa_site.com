Latest updates from the shipping and logistics sector

Ghana Shippers' Council Enhances Trade Facilitation

The Ghana Shippers' Council (GSC) has rolled out new digital platforms to streamline shipping and customs operations. This step is aimed at reducing processing time, boosting transparency, and supporting importers and exporters.

GSC Hosts Stakeholder Forum on Port Charges

GSC recently hosted a stakeholder forum in Accra to deliberate on port handling charges. Various port authorities, freight agents, and shipping companies participated to promote accountability and transparency.

Upcoming Event: Shippers’ Day Celebration 2025

The annual Shippers’ Day will be celebrated on August 10, 2025, in Takoradi. This year’s theme is “Digitalization and Sustainable Shipping for Economic Growth.” Key industry stakeholders are expected to attend.
