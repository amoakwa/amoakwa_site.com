About Ghana Shippers Council

Our History

Established in 1974 under the Ghana Shippers Council Decree (NRCD 254), the Ghana Shippers Council has played a pivotal role in representing and safeguarding the interests of Ghanaian shippers. Over the years, we've adapted to meet the dynamic needs of the maritime industry and our stakeholders.

Our Mission

To promote and protect the interests of shippers in Ghana by delivering quality services, advocating fair trade practices, and fostering an efficient shipping environment through collaborative stakeholder engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading enabler of a sustainable and competitive shipping industry that supports Ghana's economic growth and integration into the global trade ecosystem.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our engagements and decisions.

Professionalism

Our services reflect competence, dedication, and a commitment to excellence.

Innovation

We continuously seek creative and practical solutions to meet evolving challenges.
